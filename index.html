About Me

After graduating with a Bachelor of Information Technology in 2001, I've been continuously working in various software engineering roles.

My first experience was working with various Australian Government clients and large corporations, implementing and customising an in-house metadata management tool using Java, SQLServer, JSP and XSLT.

Since then I have transitioned to more web-based technologies and have specialised particularly in custom implementations of WordPress, using PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Most recently I have worked as a trainer in Software Engineering, teaching and demonstrating technologies such as HTML/CSS/Javascript, various SQL databases and React.
